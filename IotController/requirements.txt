# RASPBERRY PI 5 40-PIN GPIO HEADER LAYOUT (BCM NUMBERING)
# ============================================================================
#
# Pin Layout (20 rows x 2 columns):
#
#     LEFT SIDE              RIGHT SIDE
#     Pin  | GPIO/Function  | GPIO/Function | Pin
#     -----+----------------+----------------+-----
#      1   | 3V3            | 5V             |  2
#      3   | GPIO 2 (SDA)   | 5V             |  4
#      5   | GPIO 3 (SCL)   | GND            |  6
#      7   | GPIO 4         | GPIO 14 (TXD)  |  8
#      9   | GND            | GPIO 15 (RXD)  | 10
#     11   | GPIO 17 >PIR   | GPIO 18 >LOCK  | 12  (Solenoid Relay)
#     13   | GPIO 27        | GND            | 14
#     15   | GPIO 22        | GPIO 23        | 16
#     17   | 3V3            | GPIO 24 >BUZ   | 18  (Active Buzzer)
#     19   | GPIO 10 (MOSI) | GND            | 20
#     21   | GPIO 9 (MISO)  | GPIO 25 (RST)  | 22  (RC522 RST)
#     23   | GPIO 11 (SCLK) | GPIO 8 (CE0)   | 24  (RC522 SDA)
#     25   | GND            | GPIO 7         | 26
#     27   | GPIO 0         | GPIO 1         | 28
#     29   | GPIO 5  >LED-R | GND            | 30
#     31   | GPIO 6  >LED-G | GPIO 12        | 32
#     33   | GPIO 13 >LED-B | GND            | 34
#     35   | GPIO 19        | GPIO 16 >DOOR  | 36  (Magnetic Reed Switch)
#     37   | GPIO 26        | GPIO 20        | 38
#     39   | GND            | GPIO 21        | 40
#
# ============================================================================
# COMPONENT PIN ASSIGNMENTS (BCM GPIO NUMBERING)
# ============================================================================
# PIR Motion Sensor:     GPIO 17 (Pin 11)  > VCC: Pin 2 (5V), GND: Pin 6
# RFID RC522 Module:
#   - MOSI:              GPIO 10 (Pin 19)
#   - MISO:              GPIO 9  (Pin 21)
#   - SCK:               GPIO 11 (Pin 23)
#   - CS/NSS:            GPIO 8  (Pin 24)
#   - RST:               GPIO 25 (Pin 22)
#   - VCC:               3.3V (Pin 1) -- NOT 5V!
#   - GND:               Pin 20
# Active Buzzer:         GPIO 24 (Pin 18)  > GND: Pin 9
# Solenoid Relay:        GPIO 18 (Pin 12)  > VCC: Pin 4 (5V), GND: Pin 14
# RGB LED:
#   - Red:               GPIO 5  (Pin 29)  > 220 ohm resistor
#   - Green:             GPIO 6  (Pin 31)  > 220 ohm resistor
#   - Blue:              GPIO 13 (Pin 33)  > 220 ohm resistor
#   - Cathode:           GND (Pin 34)
# Magnetic Door Sensor:  GPIO 16 (Pin 36)  > GND: Pin 39 (INPUT PULL_UP)
# Camera Module 3:       CSI-2 Port with 22-pin ribbon cable (BLUE TAPE DOWN)
#

#   pir_sensor.py pin=17, hardware.py Buzzer pin=24, lock_sensor.py pin=18,
